Our Story

It all started with a craving. Not just for something sweet, but for just one more bite.

Among friends and family, we noticed a pattern: people hesitated when dessert came around. Not because they didn't want it, but because the portions were just too big. One person wanted a taste of brownie, another was eyeing cheesecake, and someone else just wanted a single macaron. But dessert menus didn't offer variety. You had to choose one large portion, and that was it.

So we thought... why not create a place where dessert is fun, flexible, and never too much?

One More Bite was born from that tiny wish: to let people enjoy many flavors, in mini sizes. A bite of red velvet, a nibble of tart, a swirl of cheesecake. You mix and match, build your own box, and never waste a crumb.

We are not just selling desserts. We are sharing joy, one small bite at a time.

Mission

To bring delight to every moment by crafting a variety of exquisite mini desserts that encourage mindful indulgence, spark creativity, and deliver satisfaction in every bite.

We aim to make desserts more approachable, shareable, and joy filled perfectly portioned for every craving and occasion.

Vision

To become the leading platform for customizable mini desserts, inspiring joyful moments and sweet memories, one bite at a time.
